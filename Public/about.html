About Me

I'm an aspiring developer based in Kathmandu, Nepal, currently focused on building my foundation in web development. What started as a high school subject quickly turned into something much bigger. During my first two years of high school, I was introduced to coding — and I've been hooked ever since. There was something powerful about turning logic into something visible and interactive. That feeling never left.

Right now, I’m strengthening my skills in HTML while working toward mastering CSS and JavaScript. I use GitHub and VS Code as I continue exploring different programming languages and expanding my technical range. I’m still early in my journey — but I’m building with intention and consistency.

Leadership has always been central to who I am. I’ve served as Vice President of my high school’s student council and captained my soccer team, experiences that strengthened my communication, accountability, and ability to perform under pressure. From January 2025 to June 2025, I also served as the Head of PR & Marketing at a non-profit organization in Nepal, where I led outreach initiatives, managed communications, and helped shape the organization’s public presence. That experience sharpened my strategic thinking and reinforced the importance of clear messaging and impact.

If I had to describe myself in three words: curious, adventurous, and independent. I’m driven by growth, new challenges, and the desire to build something meaningful.

Looking ahead, my goal is to pursue university studies in the United States and immerse myself in opportunities within the tech industry. I’m not just learning to code — I’m building toward long-term growth, leadership, and impact.
